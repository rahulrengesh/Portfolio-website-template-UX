About movies

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Captain America: The Winter Soldier is a 2014 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero of the same name. The film is a sequel to Captain America: The First Avenger and The Avengers. It is the ninth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the third film of Phase Two.

Avengers: Age of Ultron

Avengers: Age of Ultron is a 2015 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. The film is a sequel to The Avengers and a crossover/sequel to Iron Man 3, Thor: The Dark World, and Captain America: The Winter Soldier.

Avengers: Infinity War

Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. The film is a sequel to The Avengers, and Avengers: Age of Ultron, and a crossover/sequel to Captain America: Civil War, Doctor Strange, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. The film is a sequel to The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron, and Avengers: Infinity War
